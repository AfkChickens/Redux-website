About Us

We are REDUX, A fanmade project to bring back Recroom. As we know, Recroom's shutdown has got everybody sad, and we wanted to change that. Redux will remain free to download and play forever!

We are a small team of developers, artists, and designers who are passionate about creating a new universe for players to enjoy. Our goal is to create a game that is fun, engaging, and accessible to everyone.

Thank you for taking your time to read, it means the world to us. More development can be found in our discord server! Feel free to talk to us there and watch us as we develop this new universe.

As always, give it your REDUX BEST!
- The Redux Team

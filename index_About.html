Our Mission

We are REDUX, a fanmade project dedicated to bringing back Recroom. The shutdown left a void in the community, and we decided to change that. Redux is built on the promise to remain free to download and play forever.

The Team

We are a small but dedicated team of developers, artists, and designers. We share a common passion for creating a fun, engaging, and highly accessible universe where players can reunite and build new memories.

As always, give it your REDUX BEST!

- The Redux Team
